Objective:

The objective of Tetris is to maneuver falling blocks called Tetriminos to create horizontal lines without any gaps. When a line is completed, it clears from the playing field, allowing more space for further Tetriminos. The game ends when the Tetriminos reach the top of the playing field.

Controls:

  • Left Arrow (←): Move Tetrimino left.
  • Right Arrow (→): Move Tetrimino right.
  • Down Arrow (↓): Accelerate the falling speed of Tetrimino (soft drop).
  • Q Key: Rotate Tetrimino counterclockwise.
  • W Key: Rotate Tetrimino clockwise.

Gameplay:

  • Falling Tetriminos: Tetriminos fall from the top of the screen. You can control their movement and rotation using the arrow keys and Q/W keys.
  • Clearing Lines: When a horizontal line is completely filled with blocks, it clears from the screen, and you earn points based on the number of lines cleared simultaneously.
  • Scoring: Points are awarded for each line cleared. The more lines you clear at once, the higher your score multiplier.
  • Game Over: If the Tetriminos stack up to the top of the screen, the game ends, and your score is finalized.

Tips:

  • Plan Ahead: Anticipate the best placement for each Tetrimino to create complete lines and avoid creating gaps.
  • Use Rotation: Rotate Tetriminos to fit into tight spaces and clear lines more efficiently.
  • Down Arrow for Faster Drops: Use the down arrow to speed up the falling Tetriminos for quicker placements.
  • Stay Calm: Tetris gets faster as you progress, so stay calm under pressure and focus on clearing lines strategically.

Have Fun and Happy Tetrimino Stacking!
